About Me.

Hello! I am Sooin, a lover of fashion, design, and business. I am an enthusiastic young professional who puts all my passion into what I love. At Korea University, cheerleading and my major Fashion Design and Merchandising were what I loved most. After graduation, I worked as a fashion merchandiser in New York City. I wanted to spread beauty and provide people with meaningful experiences through products. During the Covid-19 pandemic, I saw a major shift in consumer behavior and the fashion industry turning online. Since creating my fashion brand and digital product is my long-term goal, understanding the user interface and experience was crucial. With the growing recognition of the importance of technology and human-computer interaction, I decided to be a UX designer who incorporates technology into fashion in an innovative and creative way.
You can learn more about me through this website that I designed and developed. Hope you enjoy it!
